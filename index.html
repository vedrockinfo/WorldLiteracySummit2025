WELCOME TO SYNERGY 3.0

Literacy lies at the core of all Sustainable Development Goals (SDGs) and is a paramount priority for the Government of India. The NIPUN Bharat Mission was launched with the ambitious goal of achieving universal Foundational Literacy and Numeracy (FLN) by 2026/27. Yet, as we approach the halfway point, we still grapple with realizing this vision. Could a pedagogical revolution and a unified effort make this goal attainable?

We cordially invite you to participate in Synergy Summit 3.0, from September 23-25, 2024, in Lucknow. Your presence will undeniably enrich the conversation and catalyze critical action. The summit will feature a field trip to Lucknow's government schools, where the innovative ALfA pedagogy is making significant strides in challenging the status quo.

Kindly confirm your attendance by filling out the registration form.

Literacy: A Lift Off for All SDGs

Achieving universal literacy is essential for meeting Sustainable Development Goals (SDGs). Literacy plays a crucial role in:

  • Fighting Poverty: Providing better job opportunities and economic growth.
  • Gender Equality: Empowering women and fostering equality.
  • Healthcare: Understanding health information and improving outcomes.
  • Citizen Empowerment: Encouraging active community participation.
  • Environmental Sustainability: Addressing and understanding environmental issues.
  • Civic Participation: Promoting informed and engaged citizens. The table below highlights the key interconnections between various SDGs and literacy.

Synergy 3.0 would also celebrate impactful initiatives and achievements in education, focusing on critical goals: promoting innovative pedagogical practices, enhancing access to quality education, fostering gender equality, driving innovation, and advancing equity and inclusion within learning environments. The award categories include:

  • Foundational Learning & Numeracy (FLN) Excellence Award:Recognizing exceptional dedication and success in promoting foundational literacy and numeracy skills, particularly in underserved communities.
  • Girl Education Empowerment Award:Highlighting contributions to advancing girl education and empowering girls to access quality education and reach their full potential.
  • Innovative Education Technology Award:Celebrating innovative use of technology to enhance educational outcomes, with a focus on inclusivity and equity.
  • Community Engagement in Education Award:Acknowledging initiatives that engage local communities to support education and enhance learning opportunities.
  • Educational Equity and Inclusion Award:Honoring efforts to promote equity, diversity, and inclusion in education, ensuring equal opportunities for all learners and fostering inclusive learning environments.

President & MD

City Montessori School (CMS) in Lucknow, India, proudly stands as the world's largest school, a title it has held in the Guinness World Records since 1999. Additionally, CMS was awarded the prestigious UNESCO Prize for Peace Education in 2002.

Established in 1959 by Drs. Jagdish and Bharti Gandhi, the school embraces the motto Jai Jagat, which means ‘Let glory be to the entire world’!

DEVI Sansthan—Dignity, Education, Vision International—is a global non-profit founded by Dr. Sunita Gandhi, daughter of the CMS founders. It focuses on innovative solutions to improve foundational literacy and numeracy in education worldwide.

By transforming education systems, DEVI Sansthan aims to create a brighter and more equitable future, ensuring quality education for every child.
